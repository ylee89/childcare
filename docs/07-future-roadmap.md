# 07 · Future Roadmap

A phased plan from MVP to a mature platform. Dates are relative
(post-MVP-launch) and indicative; scope per phase is the real commitment.

---

## Phase 0 — MVP (launch)
*Core loop, offline, child-safe.* See [`06-mvp-feature-list.md`](06-mvp-feature-list.md).

Deliverable: 4 core worlds (Emotion Explorer, Story Adventures, Brave Voice,
Calm Corner) + lite Empathy/Choice + lite Parent Dashboard, fully offline,
accessible, no-fail, no-addictive-mechanics.

---

## Phase 1 — Complete the core (v1.1, ~1–2 months after launch)
- **AI Story Generator** (online, parent-gated, parent-reviewed) — the headline
  fast-follow. Personalized social stories, saved offline-playable.
- **Finish Story Adventures**: add *teasing* and *taking turns* situations.
- **Finish Calm Corner**: add *Counting Calm (5-4-3-2-1)* and *Quiet Listen*.
- **Cloud sync + emailed weekly reports** (optional, parent account).
- **More emotion-recognition games** in Emotion Explorer (Feeling Sounds,
  expression matching variations).

---

## Phase 2 — Depth & personalization (v1.2–1.3, ~3–6 months)
- **Adaptive difficulty**: content gently tunes to the child's demonstrated
  level within an age band (still no scoring shown to the child).
- **Expanded Empathy Lab & Good Choice Challenge** to full depth (perspective
  swaps, richer kindness missions, more scenarios).
- **Routine builder**: parents/educators assemble a short daily "feelings
  routine" (check-in → one story → one calm activity).
- **Character growth**: the guide friends remember the child and reference past
  play warmly (no manipulation — just continuity).
- **More emotions / nuance**: secondary feelings (jealous, embarrassed, calm,
  grateful, surprised) layered on top of the core 10.

---

## Phase 3 — Educators & families (v2.0, ~6–9 months)
- **Educator / Classroom Mode**: a teacher dashboard, group-friendly activities,
  multiple-child management, SEL-curriculum alignment (e.g. CASEL framework),
  printable real-world extension activities.
- **Multi-child households**: side-by-side parent insights, sibling-safe
  profile switching.
- **Co-play / "with a grown-up" mode**: guided activities parent + child do
  together (shared breathing, story discussion prompts).
- **Localization & cultural adaptation**: priority languages + culturally
  appropriate situations, names, and VO. RTL support.

---

## Phase 4 — Ecosystem & evidence (v2.x, ~9–18 months)
- **Clinical/research collaboration**: work with child psychologists / SEL
  researchers to validate outcomes; publish efficacy. Optional
  therapist/counselor view (with consent) for kids in support programs.
- **Accessibility deepening**: switch-control support, AAC-friendly modes,
  customizable symbol sets, dyslexia-friendly options — beyond AA.
- **Content marketplace (curated, safe)**: vetted story packs (e.g. "new
  sibling," "doctor visit," "moving house," "saying goodbye to a pet"),
  themed calm scenes. Curated, never user-generated-public.
- **Smart, kind reminders**: optional, ML-informed *gentle* nudges that respect
  the no-nagging rule (e.g. suggest a calm activity at a historically tough
  time of day) — always parent-controlled, always opt-in.

---

## Always-on (every phase)
- **Safety & privacy reviews** before any feature that touches data, audio,
  network, or AI output. (See [`09-safety-privacy-accessibility.md`](09-safety-privacy-accessibility.md).)
- **Accessibility regression checks** each release.
- **No-addictive-mechanics audit**: every new feature is checked against the
  banned-pattern list in [`05-gamification-strategy.md`](05-gamification-strategy.md).
- **Child & parent usability testing** with real families in the target age
  band before shipping.

---

## Business model notes (kept out of Child Mode)
- **Freemium for families**: generous free core (the whole MVP loop free);
  premium unlocks AI Story Generator volume, story packs, multi-child, and
  advanced reports. **No ads, no IAP surfaced to children — ever.** All
  purchase flows live behind the parent gate.
- **Institutional licensing** for preschools/districts (Educator Mode).
- We will **not** monetize via engagement-maximization or data sale; the
  business depends on parent/educator trust, which the safety stance protects.

---

```
Phase 0  MVP ──────────────▶ core loop, offline, safe

Phase 4  v2.x ─────────────▶ research/evidence, deep a11y, content market
        (safety · a11y · anti-addiction · user testing run through ALL phases)
```
